Recipe for French Loaf Bread
Created and Designed By

Ingriediants

  1. 4 Cups of
  2. 100gr Self Raising Flour
  3. 4 Cups of OO Flour
  4. 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  5. 50gr of Butter
  6. 6 Eggs

Making the Dough

  • Pour the Water into a 3 litre mixing bowl
  • Siff flour gently into the water
  • Mixing the water and flour together prevent any clumps forming
  • Add the eggs, slowly mising the eggs into the batter
  • Siff in the Self Raising Flour, while gently mixing the batter clockwise
  • Add the Olive Oil
  • Continue to mix the batter until. it forms the dough

Baking Instructions

Allow Dough to the dry for a day
Baking in the oven on 150 degress for 20 min
